HOW TO MAKE A FISH

This is best accompanied with greens and ugali

  • Fried Fish
  • Cooking Oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • lemon juice
  1. Heat the oil in a pan
  2. Add the onions and cook till golden in color
  3. Add tomatoes
  4. Add salt and some pepper to season
  5. Add the lemon juice and stir for 2 seconds
  6. Put the fried fish and lit it simmer for 3 minutes
